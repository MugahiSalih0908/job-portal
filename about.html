About JobPortal

Connecting talent with opportunity since 2023

Our Mission

To democratize job searching by providing a platform where everyone has equal access to employment opportunities, regardless of their background or location.

We believe that finding the right job should be simple, transparent, and accessible to all.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted career platform, connecting millions of professionals with their dream jobs while helping companies find the perfect talent.

We envision a world where job searching is efficient, effective, and empowering for everyone involved.

Our Journey

From startup to industry leader

2023 - Foundation

JobPortal was founded with a simple mission: to make job searching easier. Started with a team of 5 in a small office.

2024 - Growth

Reached 50,000 registered users and partnered with 1,000+ companies. Expanded our team to 25 members.
